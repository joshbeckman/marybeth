Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a registry for gifts?

We have a registry with Crate & Barrel.

Can I bring a plus one?

We are asking that invited guests only bring their significant others.

Will wedding be inside or outside?

The ceremony will be held in the open-air courtyard (if weather cooperates), dinner and dancing will take place inside with opened garage doors (weather permitting).

Will there be late-night snacks at the wedding?

Of course!

Will there be an open bar at the wedding?

Of course!

What is the mask/vaccination policy?

We highly encourage getting vaccinated, but if not, we request guests to please take a COVID-19 test (cheap and available over the counter at CVS/Walgreens) within a couple days of the wedding to ensure the safety of our guests and their loved ones. We will be following state guidelines and will update here if our requirements change.

Will Marybeth or Josh be changing their last name?

Nope!

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a registry for gifts?

We have a registry with Crate & Barrel.

Can I bring a plus one?

We are asking that invited guests only bring their significant others.

Will wedding be inside or outside?

The ceremony will be held in the open-air courtyard (if weather cooperates), dinner and dancing will take place inside with opened garage doors (weather permitting).

Will there be late-night snacks at the wedding?

Of course!

Will there be an open bar at the wedding?

Of course!

Where will be party be heading after the venue?

We will need to be out of the venue by midnight, after which people will probably head over to Cobra Lounge / All Rise Brewery taproom just a couple blocks to the East.

What is the mask/vaccination policy?

We highly encourage getting vaccinated, but if not, we request guests to please take a COVID-19 test (cheap and available over the counter at CVS/Walgreens) within a couple days of the wedding to ensure the safety of our guests and their loved ones.

Following city guidelines, all guests will be required to wear masks indoors when not eating or drinking.

What is the dress code for the evening?

Formal attire that you feel good wearing!

Is there parking available at the venue?

There should be plenty of street parking around the venue and the surrounding block. There are a few parking spaces at the venue, but we would ask that they first go to older attendees or those who need to walk as little as possible.

Will Marybeth or Josh be changing their last name?

Nope!
